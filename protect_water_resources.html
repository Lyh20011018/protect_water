Why Water Matters

Water is essential for life. It supports all living organisms, regulates ecosystems, and drives the economy. Without clean and abundant water, life as we know it would not exist.

  • Life Source: Humans, animals, and plants all depend on water to survive.
  • Health Protector: Clean water prevents diseases and ensures overall well-being.
  • Economic Driver: Industries like agriculture, manufacturing, and energy rely on water.
